Artificial intelligence

If your service uses Artificial intelligence (AI), we'll need to understand and approve its use of any data provided by us.

This includes any learning or downstream usage of data provided by NHS login

We follow the National Cyber Security Centre (NCSC) definition of AI as ‘Any computer system that can perform tasks usually requiring human intelligence’.

This can include, for example:

  • text generation
  • speech recognition
  • translation between languages
  • visual perception

For more information, read the NCSC guidance on AI and cyber security.

If your service does use AI, we’ll ask you about it at your Application Review Call.
